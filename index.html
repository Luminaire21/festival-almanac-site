Privacy Policy

The Festival Almanac

Effective date: 6 February 2026

Overview

The Festival Almanac is an offline reference app dedicated to the history of The Cheltenham Festival. Your privacy is important to us, and we have designed the app with that principle at its core.

Data Collection

The Festival Almanac does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever. The app operates entirely offline and contains no analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party services.

No Account Required

The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use it.

No Network Activity

All content is bundled within the app. The Festival Almanac makes no network requests and does not communicate with any external servers during normal use.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Because the app collects no data of any kind, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date above. As the app collects no data, any future changes would relate to new features and would be reflected here before those features are released.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us on X at @RailsideLens.

The Festival Almanac is an independent project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Cheltenham Racecourse, The Jockey Club, or any official racing authority.
